FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often from our patients.

How do I book an appointment?
+

You can book via the form above, call us at +91 90429 85433, or WhatsApp us. We confirm all appointments within 2 hours during clinic hours.

Is parking available at the clinic?
+

Yes! We have free dedicated parking available for all our patients adjacent to the clinic building on Anna Salai.

Do you offer EMI or insurance options?
+

Absolutely. We offer 0% interest EMI plans through major banks and accept most major dental insurance providers. Our team will guide you through the options during your consultation.

Is the initial consultation free?
+

Yes! Your first consultation at Khalid Esthetic Smiles is completely free. Our doctor will assess your dental health and discuss a personalised treatment plan with no obligation.

What should I bring for my first visit?
+

Please bring any previous dental records or X-rays if available, your ID proof, and insurance card if applicable. Arriving 10 minutes early helps us serve you better.

Do you handle dental emergencies?
+

Yes. For dental emergencies — severe toothache, broken tooth, lost crown — WhatsApp or call us immediately at +91 90429 85433. We prioritise emergency cases and will accommodate you as quickly as possible.
